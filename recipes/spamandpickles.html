Spam & Pickles

This is almost enough Spam to get me through a week.

If you're trying to keep strict keto macros, nothing works better than Spam and pickles! The Spam is almost exactly 80% fat and 20% protein by calories, so if you add a little fat and some pickles for salt, you have a great keto meal.

This recipe uses cream cheese for added fat and olives and other garnishes for extra flavor. Don't forget to add sodium and potassium!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 can of spam
  • 1/3 block of cream cheese
  • Olives
  • Pepperoncinis
  • Sriracha
  • Salt and potassium chloride (No Salt)

Steps

  1. Open the can and take out the Spam. Cut it in half.
  2. Put half the Spam in the fridge for later. Cut the other half into three slices.
  3. Top each slice of Spam with a slice of cream cheese, some Sriracha, and olives or peppers.
  4. Top everything with salt and No Salt.
  5. Also eat a couple of pickles.
  6. Check for ketosis, my nigga.
